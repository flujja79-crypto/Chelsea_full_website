Identity & Quick Facts

Chelsea FC ("The Blues") are one of England's most recognisable clubs, competing in the Premier League and regularly in European competitions.

  • Founded: 10 March 1905
  • Stadium: Stamford Bridge (Fulham, London)
  • Owner: BlueCo
  • Chairman: Todd Boehly
  • Manager: Enzo Maresca
  • Nickname: The Blues

History & Legacy

Chelsea were founded by Gus Mears in 1905. The club rose to prominence in the 20th and 21st centuries, especially after significant investment in the 2000s which led to domestic and European success.

European Success

Chelsea won the UEFA Champions League in 2012 and 2021 and have multiple other European trophies.

Domestic Titles

The Blues have won multiple top-flight titles, FA Cups and League Cups across their history.

Global Brand

Chelsea is a global sporting brand with millions of supporters worldwide and strong commercial revenues.
